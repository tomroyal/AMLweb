Apptitude Media is proud to be the official UK representative of Mag+, a complete ecosystem for publishing designed content on tablet and smartphone devices.

Originally conceived by Berg London and launched alongside the iPad itself, Mag+ enables designers and publishers to:

  • Create beautiful, interactive content using Adobe Indesign
  • Instantly preview it on the iPad, iPhone, Kindle Fire or Android device
  • Package content for sale on any Mac or PC
  • Build a fully functional reader app with no coding required
  • Submit it for sale or free distribution on the App Store, Google Play or Kindle

Apptitude publishes several magazines using the platform, including the multi-award-winning British Journal of Photography, and offers a full range of creative services from technical support and training to contract publishing via our Apptitude Studio.

For more information, please contact Peter Stirratt on (0)20 7087 1780 or peter.stirratt@apptitudemedia.co.uk. Or download the tools for free and simply drop us a line when you're ready to publish on the App Store.
