Who We Are

Based in the heart of London, Apptitude Media was established by a team that has worked together for many years, with a portfolio that includes some of the most successful and revolutionary digital magazines in the industry. The management team comprises:

Marc Hartog, Founder and CEO

Marc has 15 years experience in publishing, in wide ranging roles from Finance Director to Publishing Director, most recently running the consumer magazine portfolio of a global media business and launching the first of their digital magazines. In early 2013 he led a Management buyout to form Apptitude Media.

marc@apptitudemedia.co.uk | @marchartog | LinkedIn

Mick Moore, Creative Director

Following 17 years as the Art Director of the British Journal of Photography, Mick has spent the last two years leading the design of bespoke digital magazines on the iPad and iPhone.

mick@apptitudemedia.co.uk

Tom Royal, CTO

With ten years' experience in technology journalism and a background in web design prior to that, Tom has led the technical development of our digital publications while editing Popular Science UK.

tom@apptitudemedia.co.uk | @tomroyal | LinkedIn

Simon Bainbridge, Editorial Director

The Editor of the British Journal of Photography for 12 years, Simon leads the editorial direction of the BJP and its sister publication Fade > Black.

simon@apptitudemedia.co.uk | @bainbridgesimon

Marc Ghione, Marketing Director

With 12 years' experience managing circulation and marketing for leading consumer magazine brands, Marc is an innovative marketer and project co-ordinator and leads our subscription sales across all platforms.

marcg@apptitudemedia.co.uk | LinkedIn
